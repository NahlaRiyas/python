MEA ENGINEERING COLLEGE

MEA Engineering College, located in Vengoor, Perinthalmanna, Kerala, is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in engineering education, innovation, and holistic development. Established in 2002, MEA is managed by the Muslim Educational Association, a registered charitable organization committed to uplifting society through quality education. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by the AICTE, New Delhi. At MEA, we strive to build not just engineers, but responsible and ethical citizens who are ready to meet global challenges. With a strong focus on academic excellence, industry interaction, research, and student empowerment, MEA stands as a beacon of knowledge and growth in the Malappuram district.
