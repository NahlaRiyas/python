Vishu

Vishu: Vishu is a Hindu festival celebrated mainly in Kerala, marking the New Year according to the Malayalam calendar. It signifies prosperity and good luck. On Vishu, people wake up early to view the Vishukkani (an arrangement of auspicious items), prepare special feasts, and burst fireworks. Vishu symbolizes new beginnings, prosperity, and good luck for the coming year. It is deeply rooted in the agricultural traditions of Kerala and celebrates the harvest season. The festival reflects hope and renewal as people pray for a bountiful year ahead.Vishu is a joyful and meaningful festival marking a fresh start filled with blessings, peace, and prosperity.
