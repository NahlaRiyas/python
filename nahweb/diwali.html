Diwali

Diwali: Also known as the Festival of Lights, Diwali is a major Hindu festival celebrated across India and other countries. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. People decorate their homes with lamps and candles, burst fireworks, exchange sweets, and perform religious rituals.Diwali is a joyful and meaningful celebration that brings families and communities together in a spirit of hope, prosperity, and happiness.
